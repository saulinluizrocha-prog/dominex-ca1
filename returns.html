60-Day Money-Back Guarantee

Every DOMINEX order is fully protected by our 60-day money-back guarantee. If you are not completely satisfied with your results for any reason, you may request a full refund within 60 days of your purchase date. No questions asked.

How to Request a Refund

1
Contact our support team within 60 days of your purchase date. Include your order number and the email address used at checkout.
2
Our team will confirm receipt of your request and provide return instructions if applicable.
3
Once your request is processed, your refund will be issued to the original payment method within 5 to 10 business days.

Conditions

The 60-day guarantee applies to purchases made through this official page only. Purchases made through unauthorized resellers are not covered by this guarantee. Refund requests submitted after 60 days of the purchase date will not be eligible.

Damaged or Incorrect Orders

If your order arrives damaged or if you receive an incorrect item, contact our support team immediately with a photo of the package. We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no additional cost to you.
