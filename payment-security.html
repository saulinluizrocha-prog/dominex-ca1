SECURITY

Payment Security

Your Payment Is Fully Protected

Every DOMINEX transaction is processed through a secure, encrypted payment environment. We take the safety of your financial information seriously, and your card details are never stored on our servers.

Security Measures

SSL Encryption All data transmitted on this page is protected by 256-bit SSL encryption. This is the same technology used by banks and major financial institutions worldwide.
Secure Payment Processors Payments are processed exclusively through trusted, PCI-DSS compliant third-party payment providers. Your card details go directly to the processor and are never visible to us.
No Stored Card Data DOMINEX does not store, retain, or have access to your credit card or payment account numbers at any time.
Fraud Monitoring Our payment partners use real-time fraud detection systems to identify and block suspicious activity before it can affect your account.

Discreet Billing

Your bank statement will show a discreet descriptor for any DOMINEX charge. The product name will not appear on your statement.

Questions About a Charge

If you see an unexpected charge or have any questions about a transaction, contact our support team with your order number. We will investigate and resolve any issue promptly.
