SHIPPING

Shipping Policy

Free Express Shipping

All DOMINEX orders ship with free express delivery. There are no hidden shipping fees at checkout. The price you see is the price you pay.

Delivery Timeframes

Processing Time: 1 to 2 business days after order confirmation
Delivery Time: 2 to 3 business days within Canada
Total Estimated: 3 to 5 business days from order date

Discreet Packaging

Every order is shipped in plain, unmarked packaging. The package label contains no reference to DOMINEX or the nature of the product. Your privacy is protected at every step.

Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with your tracking number. You can use this to monitor delivery status in real time.

Availability

DOMINEX ships exclusively to Canadian residents. Orders placed outside of Canada cannot be fulfilled through this page.

Delivery Issues

If your package does not arrive within the expected timeframe, or if it arrives damaged, please contact our support team immediately. We will investigate and resolve the issue at no cost to you.
